How to use Toxic Browser?

1. Go to the home page and type the name of the compound you want to search in the search bar.

2. Click on the search button.

3. The results will be displayed on the screen. You can find physicochemical information about compounds and their biological effects on the human body.

4. If you sign up, your search history will be saved in your user space so you don't forget anything!

5. You can also contact us if you have any questions or comments.

Enjoy your search!
